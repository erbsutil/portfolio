Mentored teams during the two-day Inovathon Sudovalley, held at ACEFB in Francisco Beltrão as part of Mundo Tech during Expobel 2026. The event immersed teams in an innovation challenge to develop practical solutions for the fair, resulting in 9 final proposals.

{/* ![Mentoring team at Inovathon Sudovalley 2026](../../assets/images/speaking/inovathon-sudovalley-team.webp) */}

My support focused on general mentoring and leveraging technology to accelerate product evolution. I helped teams use AI tools to speed up prototyping and refine their narratives for the final pitch. More than just technical advice, I pushed teams to think critically, pivot when necessary, and evolve their ideas into viable solutions based on the event's challenges.

The initiative successfully connected talent and local businesses, strengthening the regional innovation ecosystem and rewarding the top three projects that demonstrated the most impact.
